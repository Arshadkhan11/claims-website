Paradise Independent Adjusters, LLC. has advised our intent to remain open and active during the COVID-19 Pandemic.  In that regard, we will be certain to implement the following precautions while assisting our loyal clientele:

- Our adjusters will establish 24-hour contact with the insured by phone to confirm that the insureds are receptive to an in-home inspection.

- Insureds will be asked to confirm that neither they nor additional household members have not been exposed to anyone associated with international travel within the last 30 days.

- Our adjusters will confirm that no one in the insured’s household is currently ill or experiencing symptoms of COVID-19.

- All PIA team members will adhere to CDC recommendations.  All of our Adjuster's and employees are fully vaccinated.

- Our adjusters will refrain from direct physical contact with the insureds and will maintain social distancing at a minimum of 6 feet.

- Discretionary use of rubber gloves and surgical masks will be utilized as mandated. 

- Daily sanitizing is ongoing in Paradise Independent Adjusters office facilities.  This includes decontamination of workstations, electronic equipment, office supplies and the like.  

 

In these uncertain times, the safety and well-being of our clients and families is very important to us. Therefore, please remember to take all precautionary health measures, to take care of yourselves, and each other!

 

Thank you for your continued support during these troubling times.
